Our Services

Promote and Protect Publishing Rights

SPA actively advocates for the rights and responsibilities of Somali publishers, representing their interests in policy discussions with government bodies, legislators, and private entities. We strive to secure a fair and supportive environment for independent publishers, ensuring their voices are heard in all matters affecting the industry.

Foster Creativity & Literacy

We are committed to promoting a culture of reading and creativity across Somalia. SPA supports the free flow of ideas and encourages the exchange of information and opinions within the publishing sector, thereby fostering an environment where literature and education can thrive.

Industry Support & Networking

SPA provides invaluable networking opportunities by connecting Somali publishers with industry professionals and resources. Our support includes access to vital information, industry updates, and collaborative platforms that help publishers grow and excel in a competitive market.

Engage in Policy Review & Advocacy

We engage in reviewing legislative proposals that impact the publishing industry, advocating for necessary amendments to protect Somali publishers. SPA actively works to influence policy decisions that uphold the best interests of the publishing sector.

Liaise with International Bodies

SPA collaborates with international publishing organizations to advocate for copyright protection and intellectual property rights. Our work ensures that Somali publishers and authors receive the global recognition and protection they deserve.

Support Technological Advancements

We empower Somali publishers to stay ahead of industry trends by providing resources and support on new publishing technologies. SPA helps publishers adopt digital solutions and innovative practices to enhance their competitiveness and operational efficiency.

Promote Participation in Literary Events

SPA organizes and supports the participation of Somali publishers in local and international literary events, including book fairs, competitions, and exhibitions. These platforms provide publishers with opportunities to showcase their work and connect with a broader audience.

Provide Industry Support Services

SPA offers comprehensive support services, including training, workshops, and seminars tailored to the needs of Somali publishers. Our goal is to strengthen the skills and capabilities of industry professionals, ensuring the continued growth of Somalia's publishing sector.

Somalia Publishers Association (SPA) is dedicated to promoting and protecting the rights of Somali publishers. We foster creativity, literacy, and the free exchange of ideas to rebuild and nurture Somalia's publishing industry.
