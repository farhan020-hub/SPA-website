About Us

Established in the wake of Somalia's turbulent history, the Somalia Publishers Association (SPA) was formed to revive and nurture the local publishing industry. Following years of conflict that left the sector in disarray, SPA emerged as a beacon of hope for Somali publishers, advocating for their rights and fostering a resilient publishing environment.

SPA promotes creativity, literacy, and the free exchange of ideas, collaborating with government bodies to protect publishing rights. The association establishes ethical standards and guidelines, engages in policy discussions, and works with international bodies to safeguard copyright and intellectual property rights.

Through extensive support services, including training, advocacy, and networking, SPA continues to champion the growth and sustainability of Somalia's publishing sector, striving to make Somali voices heard on the global stage.

  • Promoting and protecting publishing rights.
  • Fostering creativity and literacy.
  • Establishing policy guidelines and ethical standards.
  • Engaging in policy review and advocacy.
  • Liaising with international publishing bodies.
  • Supporting technological advancements in publishing.
  • Promoting participation in global literary events.
  • Providing industry support services, training, and workshops.

Our Services

Promote and Protect Publishing Rights

Advocating for the rights of Somali publishers through representation, policy discussions, and legislative reviews to ensure a fair and supportive publishing environment.

Foster Creativity & Literacy

Encouraging a culture of reading and supporting the free flow of ideas within the publishing sector, while promoting creativity and literacy among the Somali population.

Industry Support & Networking

Providing access to a network of industry professionals, valuable resources, and industry insights, facilitating connections that drive the growth of Somali publishers.

Engage in Policy Review & Advocacy

Examining legislative proposals impacting the publishing industry and advocating for necessary amendments to protect the interests of Somali publishers.

Liaise with International Bodies

Collaborating with international publishing organizations to advocate for copyright protection and intellectual property rights, safeguarding the interests of authors and publishers.

Support Technological Advancements

Providing resources and support on new publishing technologies, enabling Somali publishers to remain competitive and maintain industry viability.

Promote Participation in Literary Events

Organizing and supporting participation in book fairs, competitions, and events that promote the reading culture and excellence in publishing.

Provide Industry Support Services

Offering training, workshops, and access to informational resources to strengthen the skills and capabilities of Somali publishers.
