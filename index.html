Why Choose Somalia Publishers Association?

Protect Publishing Rights

We advocate for the rights of Somali publishers, ensuring fair representation in policy discussions and safeguarding copyright and intellectual property rights.

Foster Creativity & Literacy

Promoting a culture of reading, creativity, and the free flow of ideas to rebuild Somalia’s intellectual and cultural heritage.

Industry Networking & Support

Access a robust network of industry professionals and resources that empower Somali publishers to thrive in the global market.

International Representation

We connect Somali publishers with international bodies like APNET and IPA, promoting participation in global literary events and book fairs.

Join SPA to Champion Somali Publishing

Become a part of a dynamic community dedicated to advancing the interests of Somali publishers. Enjoy access to industry insights, training, and international representation.

Somalia Publishers Association (SPA) is dedicated to promoting and protecting the rights of Somali publishers. We foster creativity, literacy, and the free exchange of ideas to rebuild and nurture Somalia's publishing industry.
